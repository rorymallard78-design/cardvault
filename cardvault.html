🏆 PSA Cert Lookup

Connect PSA to verify cert numbers and pull population report data for any graded card in your vault.

Get your key at psacard.com/api → Sign in → API Access → Generate Token.

🛒 eBay Market Data

Connect eBay to automatically pull recent sold prices for any card in your vault. Free eBay developer account required.

Get your key at developer.ebay.com → My Account → Application Keys → Production → App ID (Client ID).

🤖 AI Card Scanning

Add your Anthropic API key to enable AI-powered card scanning. When you take a photo of a card, Claude will automatically read and fill in all the details for you.

Get your key at platform.anthropic.com → API Keys. Each scan costs ~$0.01.

🔗 Share Collection

Generate a read-only shareable link of your collection. Anyone with the link can browse your cards but cannot make any changes.
